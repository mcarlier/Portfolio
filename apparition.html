Apparition

Look at the whole experience.

Apparition is an artistic experience which tries to immerse the users in a parallel reality where he can see the previous visitors of the installation. It tries to express that wherever you go you have an influence on your environnement. Even if you leave a place something of you can still remain inside. Project designed during my semester in the Futur University of Hakodate in Japan.
